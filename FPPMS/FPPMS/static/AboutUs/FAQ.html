Frequently Asked Questions

Who can submit a proposal?

The proposal ideas can be submitted by different organisations, ex Fanshawe students and the Fanshawe faculty

Can we upload supporting documents related to the proposal?

Yes. You can upload supporting documents, they can be in word or pdf format.

How will I know what is the current status of my project?

Once there is a change in the status, you will be approached by Fanshawe Faculty on the phone number/email provided by you.

Can one user submit more than one proposal?

Yes. A user can submit more than one proposal.

What happens after the proposal has been approved?

Students at Fanshwe will then start working on the proposal and further information will be given to you regarding that.

What if the project proposal I submit is too complex to finish in one term?

You will have an option to split the project into more than 1 parts so that it can be completed in multiple terms.

If my proposal id selected, will I be in direct contact with the students?

With every accepted proposal, one Fanshawe Faculty member will be appointed. If needed, there can be contact with the students as well.

What if I want to make changes to the proposal I have already submitted?

You can resubmit the proposal with the changes.
