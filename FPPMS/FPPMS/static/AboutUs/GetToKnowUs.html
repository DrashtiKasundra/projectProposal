Hello!


The Fanshawe Project Proposal Management System is a web-based application that permits the customers, ex-students or the clients to submit the project proposals to be considered during the approval procedure.


This platform is required by the customers and the clients in order to submit the required project proposals to the Fanshawe College students. These projects would go through approval process which would involve the Fanshawe professors and stakeholders, who would review and determine if the project can be completed by the students. The list of completed project proposals that have been submitted can be reviewed by the current Fanshawe students, professors and stakeholders.


Fanshawe college is helping the London region to provide adaptable knowledgeable arrangements and experimental education chances established in accordance to the labour market needs. The colleges are spread in Woodstock, Simcoe, London and St. Thomas, serving half a million people by engaging, educating and empowering.
